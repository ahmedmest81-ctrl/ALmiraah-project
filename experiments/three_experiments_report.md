# Three Experiments on the v3 Field — Results & Pre-registration Tally
2026-06-11 · all run on the frozen full_profiles_v3.npz matrix available for these experiments (747 terms × 99 Names) + poincare_data_v3.json. The accumulated query dataset subsequently reached 759 records.

## Exp 1 — Paired-opposite axes
**Pre-registered:** doctrinal pairs angularly distant on disk; less similar in embedding; ظلم → Muntaqim on the ʿAfw⇄Intiqām axis.
**Result:** REVERSED on geometry, HIT on decomposition. Resolved pairs (21 of 98; field is free prose, needs manual curation) sit *closer* than random (60.4° vs 88.8°), field-columns *positively* correlated (+0.180 vs +0.006), embeddings more similar. Opposites are siblings sharing one axis — jamʿ al-aḍdād made geometric. The *difference* of paired columns nonetheless yields doctrinally correct projections: موت → Mumīt (−0.164), رحمة → ʿAfw (+0.236), ظلم → Intiqām (−0.060, pre-registered hit), ولادة → Muḥyī + Taqdīm.
**Consequence:** named axes are local derivatives (column differences), not diameters. الظَّاهِر is the opposition graph's in-degree hub (4). The 98 paired_opposite prose entries need one-time manual curation into a clean table — Ahmed-level task, then the 49-axis decomposition module is mechanical.

## Exp 2 — Shadow field (tanzīh hypothesis)
**Pre-registered:** repulsion-only profiles reproduce full-profile neighborhoods better; repelled occupancy more uniform.
**Result:** FAILED both. Attraction-only Jaccard@10 = 0.451 vs repulsion-only 0.373; ginis comparable (0.628 vs 0.656).
**Consequence:** the "repelled Names are more faithful" principle was partly an artifact of the v2 hub-corrupted attraction field. Post-fix, affirmation carries more structure. Principle downgraded to: repelled sets carry independent diagnostic signal (Paper B v4 wording already correct). Curio for later: the field's common repulsion poles are الغَفَّار and الرَّؤُوف — an intense-mercy anti-hub.

## Exp 3 — Wazn × position
**Pre-registered (low confidence):** mīm-prefixed classes at larger r than bare CCC.
**Result:** NULL, decisively. Δr = −0.004 (p = 0.89); global wazn-class → r permutation p = 0.49.
**Consequence:** a strengthening null. Wazn is geometric in embedding space (Paper A) but does NOT structure field position — morphology and the Names-field are independent layers. If wazn predicted r, the coordinate system would be suspected of measuring surface form; the null is evidence the field is semantic. Belongs in Paper B §6 as a validity check.
